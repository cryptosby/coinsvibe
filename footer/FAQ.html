Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the news updated?

Our news aggregator updates every 15 minutes, pulling in the latest stories from our trusted sources. For breaking news, we manually verify and update important stories as they happen.

Where do you source your news from?

We aggregate content from reputable cryptocurrency news outlets including CoinTelegraph, Decrypt, CryptoNews, and CoinDesk. All sources are carefully selected for their reliability and journalistic standards.

Is there a mobile app available?

Currently, we don't have a dedicated mobile app, but our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile browsers. We're planning to release native apps for iOS and Android in the near future.

How can I customize my news feed?

You can use the filter buttons at the top of the news section to view only specific categories like Bitcoin, Ethereum, or NFTs. Additionally, the search function allows you to find news about specific cryptocurrencies or topics.

Are the market statistics real-time?

Our market statistics are updated every minute using data from CoinGecko's API. While not technically real-time, the data is never more than a minute old during normal operation.

How can I contact your team?

You can reach us through the contact form on our Contact page. For press inquiries, please include "PRESS" in your subject line. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
